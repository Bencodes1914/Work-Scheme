Greetings from Paris!

My Experience

I finally visited Paris this summer and it was amazing. The Eiffel Tower looked even better in person. The food was delicious, and the people were friendly. Every corner of the city had something beautiful to see.

I spent most of my days exploring the museums, walking along the Seine, and trying different pastries like croissants and macarons.

Highlights

  • Climbing the Eiffel Tower
  • Visiting the Louvre Museum
  • Boat ride on the Seine River
  • Shopping on the Champs-Élysées

Tips for Travelers
  1. Learn a few basic French phrases.
  2. Buy tickets for attractions online to skip long queues.
  3. Carry a small umbrella, Paris weather can change quickly.
